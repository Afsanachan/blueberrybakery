Saffron Kulfi

Saffron kulfi is an Indian desserts. It's made from milk and saffron. It's a delicious food. We serve it with some juicy mango. It is one of our most popular desserts.

Chocolate covered frozen banana is a Mexican dessert. We cover bananas in chocolate which is as delicious as it sounds. Then we use different toppings as per your choice. We have the following toppings--

  • White Chocolate

Peach Semifreddo

Peach Semifreddo is from Italy. Semifreddo means half frozen which you will discover after the very first bite. This dessert will make you feel you are eating flower.

Ice Scramble

It's a Philippino dessert. It's an ice based treat and it's combined with pops cereal, marshmallows and chocolate.
